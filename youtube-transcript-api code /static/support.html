Help Center

Support that saves minutes

WorthIt.AI should feel effortless. Start with the quick guides below or reach out, most questions resolve in minutes.

Get started fast

  1. Open any YouTube video and tap Share.
  2. Select WorthIt.AI in the share sheet.
  3. Wait a few seconds while we pull the transcript and comments.
  4. Skim the highlights, Worth-It Score, and ask follow-up questions.

Pro tip: add WorthIt.AI to your share favorites so it’s always one tap away.

Quick answers

Analysis failed?

Private, age-restricted, or caption-less videos are the usual culprits. Try another link to confirm.

Slow results?

Multi-hour videos or thousands of comments take a little longer. Keep the app open, we’ll notify you.

Score accuracy?

The Worth-It Score blends transcript depth, sentiment, and crowd patterns. Treat it as a compass alongside your judgment.

Troubleshooting checklist

  • Confirm your connection (Wi‑Fi or cellular).
  • Update to the latest WorthIt.AI release from the App Store.
  • Check that captions or auto captions exist for the video.
  • Relaunch WorthIt.AI if the share extension stalls.
  • Share directly from the YouTube app rather than Safari/Chrome.

Still need help?

We love detailed reports, here’s what helps us fix issues quickly:

  • The YouTube link and what you expected to see.
  • Your device model + iOS version.
  • Screenshots if cards or scores look off.
